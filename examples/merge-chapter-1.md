# Lecture 1. Foundations of Cell Biology

## 1. Introduction

A cell is the elementary unit of structure and function in all living organisms.

### Historical note

Cells were first described by Robert Hooke in 1665.

## 2. Cell structure

A cell consists of:

- Cell membrane
- Cytoplasm
- Nucleus
  - Chromosomes
  - Nucleolus

### Organelles

| Organelle | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| Mitochondria | Energy (ATP) |
| Ribosomes | Protein synthesis |
| ER | Substance transport |

## 3. Cell division

The two main types of division: **mitosis** and **meiosis**.

> **Mitosis** is the division of somatic cells in which daughter cells receive the full set of chromosomes.

Formula for the number of cells: $N = 2^n$, where n is the number of divisions.

[^note1]: Described in *Molecular Biology of the Cell* by Bruce Alberts.
