# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and constructive experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, level of experience, or technology preferences.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions.
- Assume good faith when reading other people's messages.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the wider community.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Disagree on technical merits without making it personal.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory remarks.
- Harassment in any form, public or private.
- Posting other people's private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained off-topic disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication that takes place under the project's name.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private security advisory on GitHub or by emailing the maintainer at the address listed in `SECURITY.md`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender, removing comments or commits, or banning the offender from the project.
